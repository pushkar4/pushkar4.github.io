Themes for Samsung bada Smartphones

2012 | Jamshedpur

The bada OS was Samsung's flagship OS back in the year 2012 and prior. That was when I bought the Samsung Wave II.

bada OS had a fairly less number of good themes and best ones were priced high. This led me to learn Adobe Flash CS and use the bada APIs to create my own themes for the phone. I uploaded these on the SamsungApps store and they sold like hot cakes!

Unfortunately, starting July 2014, Samsung has discontinued support for bada OS.

Featured below are 5 of the 8 paid themes I designed.



Muki

Inspired by the original Android ICS theme
This was the first theme I developed keeping all the Samsung Wave users in mind.







Vail

This theme featured a shutter effect when the phone was unlocked.







Vied

The spotlight of this one was the page flipping effect when unlocking the phone.







Arck

This was my personal favorite which I used on my Wave. The holo had to be dragged along the arc to unlock the phone. The wallpaper featured a continuous looped dreamscene.







Ajes

This theme sports a circular list of options in the UI with a tap to unlock feature.
