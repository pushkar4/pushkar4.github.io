Apptio Drive Service w/ Native Apptio app
Top 10 Finalist out of 33 teams in Apptio Hackathon Fall 2022


November, 2022 | Apptio | Bengaluru

During Apptio Hackathon Fall 2022, teamed up with 4 other members to implement Apptio Drive Service. With Apptio Helper Application, file uploads of any type are only a right click away. One can even click and upload files directly to Apptio from their smartphones in the future. The invoices in PDF/JPG/DOC form are parsed by AI/ML and converted to text intelligently using OCR and then exported to various apps. With customers now wanting to import datasets with size >2GB, we show a way to improve the user experience by automating the process into splitting the file into chunks.
